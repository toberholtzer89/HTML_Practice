Let's Raise a Roast

Coffee is something of a phenomenon. Have you ever wondered who was the brains behind the random notion that led to such a staple across so many cultures? Think about it.

1) Take a seed out of a rather "meh" cherry
2) Dry it out
3) Roast it
4) Grind it
5) Steep it in hot water and then...
6) Dump the grounds and drink the leftovers?

And yet, it's so...normal.
